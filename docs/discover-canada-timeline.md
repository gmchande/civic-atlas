# Discover Canada: Chronological Geographic Events

A complete extraction of every dated, geographically-located event from the Discover Canada study guide. Each entry is tagged with a map feature type for product planning.

**Map Feature Tags:**
- `[boundary]` — Territorial/provincial boundary change
- `[settlement]` — Founding of a settlement, fort, or colony
- `[route]` — Exploration route or journey
- `[battle]` — Military engagement
- `[political]` — Political milestone, act, or treaty
- `[infrastructure]` — Railway, canal, or major construction
- `[marker]` — Notable event tied to a specific location
- `[international]` — Event outside Canada involving Canadians
